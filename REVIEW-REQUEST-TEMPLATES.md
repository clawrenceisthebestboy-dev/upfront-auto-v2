# Up Front Auto Repair — Review Request Templates

Send these to customers after completing work. One text per customer, one time.
Pick the platform based on where they're most likely to be active.

---

## TEXT MESSAGE — Google Review (Primary — use this most)

> "Hey [Name], thanks for coming in to Up Front Auto today! If you have 60 seconds, a Google review would mean a lot to us — we're a small shop and it really helps. Here's the link: https://g.page/r/[GOOGLE_PLACE_ID]/review — No pressure either way, appreciate your business!"

**Note:** Replace [GOOGLE_PLACE_ID] with the actual Google Place ID from the Google Business dashboard.

## Tips for Getting Reviews

1. **Send within 24 hours** of completing the job — while the experience is fresh
2. **Personalize it** — use their name and mention the specific work done
3. **One ask, one time** — don't follow up. Respectful gets results.
4. **Google > Yelp** — Google reviews help local search ranking more
5. **Don't ask for 5 stars** — just ask for an honest review. Authenticity matters.
6. **Volume beats perfection** — 10 honest 4-star reviews beat 2 perfect 5-star ones
