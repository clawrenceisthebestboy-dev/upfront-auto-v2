Yarmouth Auto Repair FAQ

I commute from Yarmouth to Portland daily. How often should I rotate tires?

Every 5,000 to 7,500 miles for most vehicles. On a daily I-295 commute, that's usually twice a year. We rotate, balance, and visually inspect tires at every oil change.

My inspection sticker expires next month. Can I get it done early?

Yes. In Maine you can inspect up to a month early and the new sticker will still run a full year from the month on the old one — no lost time.

Do you offer alignments?

Yes. We recommend an alignment after any major suspension work, after hitting a significant pothole, or whenever the car pulls or wears tires unevenly.
