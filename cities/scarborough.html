How do coastal conditions affect my car in Scarborough?

Salt and humidity accelerate rust on brake lines, exhaust, and suspension components. We recommend an undercarriage inspection every spring and fall — it catches problems before they strand you.

Can I get a Maine inspection sticker even if my car has minor rust?

Maybe. Cosmetic rust doesn't fail an inspection, but structural rust on frame rails, brake lines, or subframe mounts does. We'll tell you honestly what it needs to pass.
