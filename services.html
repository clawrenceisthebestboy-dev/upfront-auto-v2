What We Do

Oil Change & Maintenance

Conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic oil changes. We handle all routine maintenance to keep your vehicle running smoothly and extend engine life.

Brake Repair & Service

Pads, rotors, calipers, brake lines, and fluid flushes. If your brakes are squealing, grinding, or feel soft, bring it in — we will get you stopping safely.

Engine Diagnostics

Check engine light on? We use professional-grade scan tools to pinpoint the problem accurately before any wrench turns. No guesswork, no unnecessary repairs.

Tires & Balancing

Tire supply, installation, rotation, and balancing. We help you find the right tires for your vehicle and Maine's roads — all seasons and winter-rated.

Exhaust & Muffler

Muffler replacement, exhaust pipe repair, catalytic converter work, and custom exhaust fabrication. We fix leaks and get rid of that loud rumble.

Electrical & Battery

Battery testing and replacement, alternator repair, starter issues, and electrical diagnostics. We track down shorts, drains, and wiring problems.

Transmission Service

Transmission fluid changes, filter replacement, and diagnostic work. Catching transmission issues early saves you from costly rebuilds down the road.

Heating & A/C

Heater core, blower motor, A/C recharge, compressor repair, and climate control diagnostics. Stay comfortable through Maine winters and summers.

Suspension & Steering

Shocks, struts, ball joints, tie rods, control arms, and wheel alignment. If your ride feels rough or your steering pulls, we will sort it out.

State Inspection (Maine)

Licensed Maine state inspection station. We will inspect your vehicle and let you know exactly what it needs to pass — no pressure, just honest answers.

Welding & Custom Fabrication

Rust repair, frame patching, exhaust fabrication, and custom metal work. Maine roads are tough on vehicles — we weld what others would scrap.
