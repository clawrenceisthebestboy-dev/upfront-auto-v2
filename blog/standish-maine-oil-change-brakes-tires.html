Brake Repair

Maine winters are brutal on brakes — salt, sand, moisture, and freeze-thaw cycles chew through pads and rotors fast. Don't wait until you hear grinding. Signs you need brakes:

  • Brake pedal feels soft or spongy
  • Car pulls to one side when braking
  • Vibration in the steering wheel when braking
